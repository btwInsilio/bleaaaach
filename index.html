THOUSAND YEAR BLOOD WAR



The Final Arc

The plot of the 17th season of "Bleach" will develop after the previous season of 2012, turning into an adaptation of the source material of the story arc of the same name. Last season, the presence of the Quincy was established, who were the eternal enemies of the Shinigami, forced to hide after they were defeated.
